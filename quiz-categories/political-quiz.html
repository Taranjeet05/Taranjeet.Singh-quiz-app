Political Quiz

1. What is the term for a system where power is divided between a central government and individual states?

Answer: Federalism

2. What political ideology emphasizes individual liberties and limited government?

Answer: Liberalism

3. Which political system is characterized by a single-party rule?

Answer: Totalitarianism

4. What is the process called where citizens vote directly on laws and policies?

Answer: Referendum

5. Which economic system advocates for public ownership of the means of production?

Answer: Socialism

6. Which political ideology seeks to maintain traditional institutions and values?

Answer: Conservatism

7. What term describes a government ruled by a king or queen?

Answer: Monarchy

8. What is the term for a government ruled by religious leaders?

Answer: Theocracy

9. What is the term for a government where power is held by a small group of people?

Answer: Oligarchy

10. What term is used for a system of government where there is no government at all?

Answer: Anarchy
